We live in a world, where students are expected to choose a career based on what would look good in their parents’, and society’s eyes.
Students try to meet the, unrealistic expectations set by society for them, and choose, a wrong path to walk on.
But deep-down, students know what they are passionate about.
But for them, following their passion might not be accepted by their parents and society, as they think they might look down on them.
This would push them into confusion, and fear, of making a wrong decision.
Making this wrong decision, might get them down to the path of depression and boredom.
Where, they will lose all their enthusiasm, and curiosity, and will lead, their life in pure apathy.
At this stage, what a student needs is proper guidance, and motivation.
The student needs to be guided, in his area of interest, so that he becomes confident enough to fight the world, and follow his passion.
We want to take this initiative, to guide, and teach them virtually, and bring more creative, talented, and passionate people into the light.
